The series of Monster Hunter is about capturing, trapping, and defeating monsters which drop an assortment of items and materials that can be used to craft armor, gear, and supplies to aid in hunting stronger monsters. Monster Hunter Wilds is the sixth installment in the series and features an ever changing enviroment that affects the difficulty of each hunt and what monsters that might appear in the world.
